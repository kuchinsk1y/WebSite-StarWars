Darth Vader is a fictional character in the Star Wars franchise. He was first introduced in the original film trilogy as one of the leaders of the Galactic Empire. The prequel trilogy chronicles his transformation from the Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker into the Sith Lord Darth Vader.

Din Djarin, known as The Mandalorian, or Mando for short, is a fictional character in the Star Wars franchise, who appears as the titular protagonist of the Disney+ television series The Mandalorian and also appears in its spin-off The Book of Boba Fett.

Imperial Stormtroopers are characters in the Star Wars franchise, warriors of the Galactic Empire. Like the Star Destroyers and TIE fighters, stormtroopers represented the Emperor's power, his iron will, and his use of intimidation to keep thousands of star systems in line.

Din Grogu (known as Grogu before his adoption by Din Djarin) was a Force-sensitive Mandalorian male who was of the same unknown species as Grand Master Yoda. Grogu was born around 41 BBY, and raised in the Jedi Temple on Coruscant as a Jedi Initiate in the Jedi Order.
